The King of the Hill 9 ROYALE – “Durham’s Phantom Thieves” was a competitive esports tournament held in Durham, England, on March 15, 2025. Organized by the Durham Smash Ultimate community, it featured a variety of events, including Ultimate Singles, Rivals of Aether, and Team Battles. The tournament attracted a diverse group of players and spectators, showcasing both local talent and regional competitors.
The event was held at Durham University’s Student Union, providing a vibrant and accessible venue for attendees. The atmosphere was charged with excitement as players competed in a double-elimination bracket format, ensuring that each participant had a fair chance to showcase their skills. Matches were streamed live, allowing fans to follow the action online.
The tournament was a significant event in the Durham esports calendar, fostering community engagement and providing a platform for players to compete at a high level. The success of King of the Hill 9 ROYALE has set the stage for future events, with organizers aiming to continue building on this momentum and offering more opportunities for competitive gaming in the region.

At King of the Hill 9 ROYALE, a standout performance came from a Greninja player known as Command.
Command made an impressive and thrilling run through the bracket, showcasing exceptional skill and game sense.

One of the highlights of his journey was a clutch victory over JBell, a top tier Ganondorf player known for his powerful and aggressive playstyle.

Command’s strategic use of Greninja’s speed and versatility allowed him to outmaneuver JBell’s heavy hitting attacks, turning the tide in intense matches.

This upset was a key moment in the tournament and helped propel Command to a remarkable 7th place finish-a fantastic achievement that earned him recognition and respect from the community.

His run was a true display of determination and talent, proving that with the right mix of skill and strategy, underdog players can shake up the competition and leave a lasting impression.
